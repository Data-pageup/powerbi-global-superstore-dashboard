##  Key Insights

- Technology generates the highest sales among all categories.
- Consumer segment contributes more than 50% of total sales.
- Sales and profit show consistent growth from 2011 to 2014.
- Certain regions significantly outperform others in profitability.
- Higher discounts tend to reduce overall profit.
- Standard shipping accounts for the largest share of sales volume.
